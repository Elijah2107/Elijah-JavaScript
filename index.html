You create a minecraft world

You punch wood at the start of the game

Next you craft them into planks and use 4 planks to craft a crafting table

After that you craft sticks and craft a wooden pick with three planks and two sticks

Find a cave and mine stone and you will receive cobblestone

Craft some stone tools using that cobblestone and sticks

build a starter home using either dirt or wood

break some grass and you will receive wheat seeds

Til the ground using your hoe and plant the wheat seeds

Head back down into the cave

Mine some coal and craft some torches and save some of them

Find iron, mine it and receive raw iron
